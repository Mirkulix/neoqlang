# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a friendly, safe and welcoming environment for all.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Focus on constructive feedback.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Scope
This applies to all community spaces and interactions within this repository (issues, discussions, PRs) and external channels officially managed by maintainers.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, email the maintainers at: security@qlang.dev. Reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take appropriate action, including warnings and temporary or permanent bans, at their discretion.
